Services

We're Here to Help!

Tours

Trail Tours guides are available to take you on rides in the Spencer, Kelly Canyon, and Swan Valley areas. Our guides are well informed on the best trails and know what’s suitable for every skill level. They can show you and your group all that these wonderful places have to offer. To speak with a guide and make a reservation, give us a call!

Lessons

Interesting in learning how to ride a motorcycle or ATV? Look no further! Along with guided adventures, Trail Tours offers lessons to help show you the ropes and get you started. Our guides and instructors know the best places to learn and hone your skills so you can be ready for your first adventure. Contact us for more information and to plan your first lesson.

Important Note: For riders with little to no experience, a lesson is required before being allowed on their first tour.

Rentals

If you want to give trail riding a try, but don’t want to spend thousands of dollars, Trail Tours has a few vehicles and safety equipment available to rent.

Vehicle Rentals

To rent a vehicle, call or email us to set up your rental appointment. At your rental appointment you’ll be given a rental contract and waivers to go over and sign. The rental contract and waiver include usage agreements and commitments to pay for any damages. The price to rent is $50.00 per hour. You are responsible for transporting the vehicles.

Equipment Rentals

All riders, no matter the vehicle type, are required to wear a DOT approved helmet. We have a variety of styles and sizes to choose from. We require all dirt bike riders to wear riding boots. If you don’t own boots, we have some available to rent. If you do not own proper safety equipment, contact us and set up your rental appointment. You will be required to sign a rental contract that includes usage agreements and commitments to pay to repair or replace any damaged equipment.
